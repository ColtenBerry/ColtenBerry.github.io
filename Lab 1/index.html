Throne, Teeth, Trousers

Throne

Thrones have been used throughout time to display power. They are pretty epic!

  • Used exclusively by people
  • Seen as a sign of great wealth and power
  • Used for sitting upon

Teeth

Teeth are found on just about every animal on the planet. They are certainly not unique to humans and their widespread use is testament to their necessity for the survival of not only humans, but the vast majority of animals on the planet

  • Used by both animals and people
  • If properly maintained, they will be white
  • Used for chewing food as well as attacking or defending oneself

Trousers

Trousers are something that, as far as I know, are completely unique to humans. However, their widespread use across cultures, time, and geographic location show their necessity and sheer usefulness.

  • Used exclusively by people
  • They come in different colors, sizes, and varieties
  • They are used as both a means of insulation as well as being stylish
